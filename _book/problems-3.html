Figure 9.13: Metabolic rates of all squirrels used in this study as a function of effective ambient temperature. Open circles represent metabolic rates of resting squirrels at effective ambient temperatures below the thermal neutral zone. Open squares represent meta bolic rates of exceptionally active squirrels, or those whose body temperature was falling below normal levels.
